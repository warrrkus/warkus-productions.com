Description

House of Necrosis is a turn-based, grid based horror RPG, inspired by the early 3D Survival Horror games of the 90s. A special forces team went missing while investigating bizarre murder cases in an old mansion near the forest. You were sent to discover why.

History

House of Necrosis started development in 2022 as part of the 7DRL challenge and is the first commercial game by Warkus. A very early prototype / demo was released as part of the Haunted PS1 Demo Disc: Spectral Mall in 2023. The steam demo was released in 2025 where House of Necrosis was also part of the Steam Next Fest.

Features

  • Level up your character and upgrade your weapons while making your way through a house of horror
  • Wander through the endless halls of the mansion that change every time you enter
  • Inspired by the 32-bit classics, House of Necrosis looks and feels like your favorite 90s games
